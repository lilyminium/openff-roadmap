## Goals

- Define a representative library of nucleic acid fragments covering DNA and RNA backbone segments (phosphate-sugar-base units), nucleobase analogues, base-stacking model systems, perhaps others
- Submit and complete high-level QM calculations via QCSubmit
- Validate, curate, and publicly deposit the dataset in QCArchive for use by OpenFF and the broader biomolecular simulation community

## Benefits

This is a necessary first step towards our planned extension towards nucleic acid parameters.
