Roadmap Proposal 2026–2027

Staff recommendations and alternatives for the Open Force Field Consortium's 2026–2027 priorities. Solid-border nodes are recommended items; dashed-border nodes are alternatives. Recommendations are made based on staff's estimation of cost-benefit-risk analysis of individual projects, without any effort to match recommendations to available capacity. If all recommended priorities were pursued, they would amount to a drastic over-commitment of staff effort. Final decisions on the roadmap, including fitting objectives to staff capacity, will be made by the OpenFF Consortium Governing Board. Click any project to view details.
